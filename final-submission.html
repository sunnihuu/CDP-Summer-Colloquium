Precendent Study:
The Observatory of Economic Complexity (OEC)

Final Submission

1. Methodology

Software: OEC uses interactive, open-source web tools for exploring global trade data through treemaps, networks, and rankings. The focus is on clarity and accessibility.

Data: Aggregates and cleans trade data from UN Comtrade, World Bank, and others, maintaining clear provenance and regular updates.

Theory: Applies economic complexity theory, using network and mathematical models to reveal knowledge intensity and economic structure beyond GDP.

2. Rhetorical Analysis

  • Visualizes economic complexity to democratize data access.
  • Challenges GDP as the sole metric; highlights deeper economic structures.
  • Links development to product diversity and sophistication.
  • Promotes transparency and critical thinking about global trade.

Criticality: OEC exposes hidden economic structures, critiques traditional reporting, and encourages a systemic view of economies and inequalities.
